# Customer Communication Policy

These rules apply to customer-facing preliminary loan-assessment messages in
this demonstration application.

Customer messages must be clear, respectful, concise, and understandable to a
non-technical audience.

Messages may identify whether an application received a favorable or
unfavorable preliminary assessment.

For favorable preliminary assessments, the message may use warm language such
as "Congratulations" or "You are one step closer," provided that the wording
does not imply final approval, guaranteed financing, confirmed funding, or
approved loan terms.

For unfavorable preliminary assessments, the message must use respectful and
neutral language. It must not imply that submitting additional documents or
information is likely to reverse the assessment unless the workflow explicitly
states that additional information is required.

Customer messages must not mention machine learning, artificial intelligence,
algorithms, model names, probabilities, confidence levels, or risk scores.

Messages must not expose sensitive applicant attributes, including age, sex,
marital status, personal status, or foreign-worker status.
