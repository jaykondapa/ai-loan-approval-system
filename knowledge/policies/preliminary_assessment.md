# Preliminary Assessment Workflow Policy

The credit-risk model produces a preliminary decision-support result only.

A favorable preliminary assessment is not a final loan approval.

An unfavorable preliminary assessment is not presented as a final legal or
lending decision.

The system does not determine or confirm:

- approved loan amount;
- interest rate;
- repayment schedule;
- funding date;
- loan product;
- final lending terms.

For favorable preliminary assessments, customer communication may explain that
submitted information could be verified and that supporting documentation may
be requested before a final decision.

For unfavorable preliminary assessments, customer communication should state
that the application did not receive a favorable preliminary assessment and
that further communication will follow if required.

The message must not promise that the institution will provide financing.
